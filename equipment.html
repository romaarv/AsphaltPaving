Rental Opportunities

Road equipment rental is the optimal solution for those seeking to complete their construction or renovation projects efficiently and resource-savingly. AsfaltBud provides road equipment rental services, enabling you to equip your project with modern and reliable equipment.

Road Milling Machine

Renting a road milling machine allows you to quickly and efficiently remove old asphalt pavement, preparing the surface for subsequent repairs or paving. Our equipment ensures high milling precision and quality.

Road Roller

Renting a road roller is essential for compacting and leveling asphalt surfaces. We offer high-quality rollers with varying load capacities, ensuring uniform and durable compaction.

Asphalt paver

Renting an asphalt paver offers professional asphalt paving at a minimal cost. Our equipment allows you to control the speed and quality of asphalt paving, ensuring a smooth and uniform surface.

Dump truck

Renting a dump truck is essential for transporting construction materials and waste. Our dump trucks offer high load capacity and reliability, ensuring efficient logistics on the construction site.
