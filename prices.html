The price of asphalt paving depends on many factors that influence the final cost

  • The total area of the section to be paved. The larger the area, the lower the cost of the work
  • The type of asphalt used. Different types of asphalt have different costs. For example, asphalt concrete is more expensive than asphalt aggregate
  • Availability of additional work. If additional work is required, such as foundation preparation and excavation work
  • Seasonality. In winter, the cost of asphalt paving may be higher, as it is a more complex and labor-intensive process

Below we provide a price list for each type of work. If you would like a more precise estimate for asphalt paving, please contact us.

Stages of road paving

  • Asphalt Laying: This key stage involves professionally laying the asphalt mixture onto the surface (from €10 for m²)
  • Substrate preparation: This step is essential for creating a strong and stable base for the asphalt. This ensures the stability of the pavement and prevents future deformation (from €5 for m²)
  • Excavation: Our team carefully prepares the area, removing unnecessary materials and creating optimal conditions for laying asphalt (from €5 for m²)

Each of these stages is an important link in the chain of creating high-quality asphalt pavement, ensuring the durability, reliability, and safety of road infrastructure.
